Чи зможу я реально змінитись за 6 днів? Can I really change in 6 days?

You won't change everything in 6 days — and that's not the goal. The goal is to give the nervous system a new experience of safety and regulation. The body now knows this state exists. Then the Architect phase works for the following year.

This is not for those seeking "rest" or a "beautiful experience." This is for those ready to work with their system seriously and long-term. If you just want nice ocean photos — there are other places.
Чому лише 6 людей? Why only 6 people?

В групі з 6 людей активно працюють дзеркальні нейрони і відбувається co-regulation — синхронізація нервових систем. Більша група руйнує цей процес. 6 — це точне число. In a group of 6, mirror neurons actively work and co-regulation occurs — nervous system synchronization. A larger group destroys this process. 6 is the precise number.
